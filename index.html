Empower a Woman Today: To Break the Cycle of Poverty

At Renbi Women Empowerment Initiative, we are passionate about finding lasting solutions. We empower women from disadvantage communities in Nigeria by providing them training, mentorship and financial support.

About Us

At Renbi Women Empowerment Initiative, we prioritize the advancement of women and firmly believe that empowering women is an absolute necessity in creating a truly equitable and just society.

Our Mission

To equip Nigerian women with the resources necessary to attain financial independence and reach their full potential.

Our Vission

To create sustainable economic opportunities for women in disadvantaged communities in Nigeria.

Our Aims & Objectives

To support women in taking control of their finances and making smart financial decisions that benefit themselves and their families.

What we do

At Renbi Women Empowerment Initiative, we are dedicated to empowering women in vulnerable communities across Nigeria. Our goal is to equip them with the resources and support necessary to achieve financial independence and unlock their full potential through our initiatives and programs.

Why Renbi?

Investing in the empowerment of women has the potential to create a positive ripple effect that extends beyond their own lives and benefits their families, communities and society at large.

This wide-reaching impact highlights the crucial importance of prioritizing women's empowerment as a means of building a better world.

Advancement of Women

At Renbi Women Empowerment Initiative, we prioritize the advancement of women. We firmly believe that empowering women is an absolute necessity in creating a truly equitable and just society.

Our mission is to equip underprivileged women in Nigeria with resources to launch or expand their businesses and vocational training to enhance their skills.
